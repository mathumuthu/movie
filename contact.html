Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send you a reset link via email.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Account Settings > Subscription > Cancel Subscription. You can continue watching until the end of your billing period.

Why is my video buffering?

Check your internet connection speed. We recommend at least 5 Mbps for HD streaming and 25 Mbps for 4K content.

Can I download content to watch offline?

Yes! Click the download icon next to any title. Downloads are available on mobile apps and tablets.

How many devices can I use?

Depending on your plan, you can stream on 1-4 devices simultaneously and register up to 6 devices.

Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any issues.
